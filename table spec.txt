Date – This provides the date as an integer where 20100527 would represent May 27th, 2010. Time – This gives the time as an integer where 1426 would represent 2:26PM EST.Open – The open price.High – The high price.Low – The low price.Close – The close price.Volume – The trading volume during the interval. Note that it is extremely difficult to get accurate volume information. The volume is adjusted for splits so that the total value of shares traded remains constant even if a split occurs.
